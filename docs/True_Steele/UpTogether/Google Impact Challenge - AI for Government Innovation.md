# PROGRAM OVERVIEW

A $30M global initiative to help governments use AI to improve public services. Seeking nonprofits, social enterprises, and academic institutions partnering with governments and using generative and agentic AI to transform how public services function for social good.

**FOCUS AREAS**  
**Health:**  
Enhance public systems so more effective healthcare services can reach all citizens regardless of location.  
**Resilience:**  
Empower communities to plan, respond, and recover from crises via AI-powered forecasting and real-time data.  
**Economy:**  
Expand opportunity and affordability by improving public infrastructure using agentic AI and predictive optimization.

**EVALUATION CRITERIA**  
**Impact:**  
Data-driven solutions with measurable outcomes, strong government partnerships, clear beneficiary reach.  
**Innovative Use of Technology:**  
Generative or agentic AI beyond incremental change, aligned with Google AI Principles.  
**Feasibility:**  
Realistic execution plan, capable team, documented government buy-in, clear metrics.  
**Scalability:**  
Replication potential across governments and regions; plan for knowledge-sharing beyond Google.org support.
