## What We'll Do Together

### The Core Sprint: AI Strategy + Google.org Application

This is the foundation regardless of which option you choose.

**AI Strategy for UpTogether**
- Audit your current platform, data assets, and technical capabilities
- Identify 3-5 high-leverage AI use cases (predictive family-to-resource matching, agentic case management, NLP on family narratives for policy evidence, automated government reporting)
- Prioritize by impact, feasibility, and Google.org challenge alignment
- Deliver a clear AI roadmap that serves both the application and your broader tech direction

**Google.org Application**
- Draft the full application with insider-informed positioning
- Frame your government partnerships for maximum evaluator impact
- Position your AI vision as transformative, not incremental (this is what separates winners from the pile)
- Address team capability and feasibility head-on
- Review and polish with your team before submission
