**THE SITUATION**

Camelback Ventures has invested in 200+ entrepreneurs of color across 16 cohorts. Your team cares deeply about what happens after the fellowship ends, but tracking alumni ventures at scale is a manual, time-consuming process. Staff check LinkedIn, scan news sites, piece together funding announcements, and try to keep a mental model of which founders are thriving, which need support, and which have pivoted or closed.

The result is predictable: the alumni you hear from stay visible, while the ones who go quiet often need the most help. Important signals get missed. Milestone celebrations arrive late. Risk flags surface too slowly to act on.

You need a system that does this automatically: monitor every alumni venture, surface what matters, and give your team a clear picture of the full portfolio every week.

**WHAT WE'LL BUILD**

A live Alumni Intelligence Dashboard that tracks your full portfolio of alumni ventures and delivers a weekly briefing your team can act on.

**The system will:**

- Scrape all Camelback alumni LinkedIn profiles and posts weekly
- Run automated web searches for news coverage, funding announcements, and public milestones
- Score each venture on activity level, momentum direction, and engagement
- Flag risk signals: declining activity, negative press, leadership changes, prolonged silence
- Present everything in two views: a detailed table for day-to-day tracking and a dashboard for portfolio-level patterns

**Detailed View** — A sortable, filterable table showing every alumni venture with:

- Venture stage (pre-seed through growth, acquired, closed)
- Funding raised and revenue range
- Team size and growth
- LinkedIn activity (posts, engagement rate, follower trends)
- News mentions with sentiment
- Camelback engagement score
- Updates and momentum indicators
- Click-through to a full venture profile with LinkedIn posts, news articles, milestones, and your team's notes

**Dashboard View** — A portfolio-level summary showing:

- Total ventures, total capital raised, aggregate momentum
- Portfolio distribution by stage and sector
- Most active and top-funded leaderboards
- Recent news feed across the portfolio
- Needs Attention alerts (declining ventures, risk flags, inactive founders)

**Weekly Briefing** — An automated email digest each Monday with:

- New funding announcements and milestones
- LinkedIn posts worth celebrating or sharing
- Risk flags and ventures needing outreach
- Portfolio health summary
