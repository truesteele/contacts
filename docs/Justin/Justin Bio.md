Justin Steele is Co-Founder and CEO of Kindora, a Public Benefit Corporation democratizing professional fundraising tools for every nonprofit. At Google.org, he directed $700 million in philanthropic grants over a decade and currently serves as a trustee of The San Francisco Foundation. But when he and his wife Sally launched their own nonprofit—Outdoorithm Collective, which takes urban families on transformative camping adventures—they couldn't afford the very fundraising tools that would help them succeed. So he coded something better with AI. A Harvard MBA/MPA with an undergraduate engineering degree who started his career at Bain and Bridgespan, Justin now combines big-company experience with startup grit to ensure every good idea has a fair shot at success. Sally and Justin are raising their four daughters in Oakland, CA.
