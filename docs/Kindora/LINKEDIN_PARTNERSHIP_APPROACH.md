## 3. Shining Examples of LinkedIn Partnerships

### CoorDown "Hiring Chain" (2021)
LinkedIn's most prominent nonprofit content partnership. For World Down Syndrome Day, LinkedIn partnered with CoorDown (Italian disability nonprofit) and Grammy-winning Sting to create a global awareness campaign. **LinkedIn funded the project, produced it, and amplified it across their platform.** The campaign generated massive global press. This shows LinkedIn will invest significantly when a partnership aligns with their mission and creates a compelling narrative.

### LinkedIn x Comcast NBCUniversal (2025)
LinkedIn partnered with NBCUniversal to support four workforce development nonprofits (The Knowledge House, RE:IMAGINE ATL, Hopeworks, Veterans Leadership Program). The deal combined NBCUniversal's Creative Impact Lab (PSA production) with LinkedIn's Ad Grants (free advertising). PSAs aired across Comcast NBCUniversal platforms AND were elevated through LinkedIn Ad Grants across their 1.3B member community.

### Year Up (11+ year partnership)
LinkedIn's longest-running nonprofit partnership. 243 Year Up interns hosted at LinkedIn. Year Up is featured prominently across LinkedIn's social impact materials.

### Global Mentorship Initiative
Included in LinkedIn's Global Impact Partner Program — shows LinkedIn's willingness to partner with mission-aligned tech-for-good platforms.

### Future of Work Fund 2025 Recipients
- **Karya** (AI-powered livelihood platform for rural India)
- **CareerVillage.org** (AI career guidance for underrepresented youth)
- **Beyond12** (AI student success for first-gen college students)
All received $200-300K + in-kind LinkedIn support. **These are AI + social impact platforms — exactly Kindora's profile.**

## 6. Proposed Partnership Framework — Good / Better / Best

### GOOD: Content Amplification
**What Kindora does:** Publishes daily grant opportunity posts to our Kindora LinkedIn page
**What LinkedIn does:** Occasional reposts/amplification from the LI4NP page; helps resolve the Community Management API access issue

**Value to LinkedIn:** Free, high-quality nonprofit content that keeps professionals coming back to the platform daily. Kindora becomes a content engine for the nonprofit vertical.

**Lift for Kavell:** Low. Just needs to get API access approved and connect us with LI4NP page admins for occasional reposts.

### BETTER: Content Partnership + Ad Grants
Everything in GOOD, plus:
- **Kindora applies for LinkedIn Ad Grants** ($50K-$200K in free LinkedIn ads) to amplify the daily grant content and drive nonprofit signups
- **LI4NP features Kindora** in a case study and/or Better Together fireside chat
- **Co-branded weekly series**: "This Week in Grants" posted from Kindora but featured on LI4NP newsletter
- **LinkedIn connects Kindora with Heather Combs/GMI** for formal content partnership

**Value to LinkedIn:** Attracts new nonprofit users to the platform (Kindora's free tier means more nonprofits signing up for LinkedIn to engage with grant content). Content drives GMI's nonprofit engagement metrics. Easy win for Heather as she shapes GMI's new strategy.

**Lift for Kavell:** Medium. Needs to make introductions to Heather/GMI and sponsor the Ad Grants application.

### BEST: Strategic Platform Partnership
Everything in BETTER, plus:
- **Future of Work Fund application** ($200K-$300K, apps open through March 15, 2026) — Kindora uses AI to democratize grant access, directly in the "AI + workforce development" lane
- **LinkedIn integrates Kindora data** into nonprofit-focused features (e.g., Kindora's funder data enriches LinkedIn Sales Navigator results for nonprofits doing prospect research)
- **Co-branded "Grant of the Day" feature** on LinkedIn's platform for nonprofit users
- **Justin becomes a LinkedIn for Nonprofits advisory voice** — speaking at Better Together events, contributing to the Resource Hub

**Value to LinkedIn:** Kindora becomes part of LinkedIn's product story for nonprofits. "LinkedIn + Kindora = the complete nonprofit professional toolkit." Drives product adoption (Sales Navigator) among nonprofits. Creates a defensible narrative for GMI's value.

**Lift for Kavell:** Higher. Needs executive sponsorship from Heather and alignment with product teams. But the payoff is a signature partnership that justifies GMI's existence.
