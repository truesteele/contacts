The Opportunity

Kindora seeks a content partnership with LinkedIn for Nonprofits and Global Impact Marketing to provide daily, high-value grant intelligence content that gives nonprofit professionals a reason to visit LinkedIn every day. Kindora produces the content. LinkedIn gets increased daily member value (DMV), nonprofit engagement, and a pipeline to LinkedIn Talent Solutions and Sales Navigator adoption.

What is Kindora

AI-powered grant intelligence platform that helps nonprofits find the right funders, write stronger grants, and practice their pitch. Founded by a former Google.org Americas lead who directed $698M in philanthropic investments. Public Benefit Corporation.
