## Strategic Context

### Why Camelback is the founding partner
- Kindora is a Camelback Fellow (Cohort 16, Feb 2026)
- Camelback backs underrepresented founders in education and social impact — precisely Kindora's mission
- 30 fellows = instant distribution + structured enablement
- Camelback already runs workshops, office hours, etc. — adoption infrastructure exists
- Shawna Young (CEO) tagged as enterprise buyer prospect with growing relationship

### Why 15% (not deeper)
Elaine explicitly requested top-of-market pricing:
- Every future deal at 20%+ feels like a win: *"Our founding partner pays 15%. At your volume, we offer 20%."*
- Camelback gets founding enterprise partner positioning — that's the value exchange
- Prevents the early-stage trap of a low anchor that permanently erodes margins

### Reference rights
Camelback can be cited as anchor partner and pricing benchmark in future enterprise negotiations:
- "Camelback Ventures, a leading accelerator for founders of color, selected Kindora as their enterprise grant intelligence platform"
- Aggregate adoption/usage stats (with Camelback approval)
- Not individual fellow data or org-specific outcomes (privacy boundary)
