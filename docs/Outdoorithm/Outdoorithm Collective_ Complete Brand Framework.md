## **Outdoorithm Collective: Complete Brand Framework**

**Why it matters:** You now have a clear, simple way to talk about what you do that leads with universal human needs while keeping all your justice and transformation outcomes intact.

---

### **THE BRAND ARCHITECTURE**

**30,000 ft \- WHY (The Problem \+ Vision):**

**The Crisis:** America's isolation epidemic—families living in screens and silos, disconnected from nature, disconnected from each other, especially across race and class.

**Our Vision:** Reconnecting families to the land and to each other. What looks like a camping trip is actually a practice of reclaiming what modern life has severed.

---

**10,000 ft \- WHAT (The Solution):**

**The 5 Circles** \- We organize experiences around 5 interconnected dimensions of wellbeing:

1. **Rest** \- Deep sleep, restoration, unplugging from screens  
2. **Nourishment** \- Shared meals, breaking bread, eating together  
3. **Play** \- Physical freedom, movement, embodied joy  
4. **Sacred Space** \- Rituals, ceremonies, threshold moments  
5. **Public Lands** \- Reconnecting to nature, reclaiming outdoor spaces

**All experienced in community across race and class**

This is the transformation families experience and take home.

---

**3 ft \- HOW (The Method):**

**The 5 Mantras** \- Practical wisdom that guides the experience:

1. **Leave Anyway** \- Take the first step; action creates momentum  
2. **Camp As It Comes** \- Embrace surrender, not control  
3. **Nobody Solos** \- We need each other; isolation to interdependence  
4. **Dirt Don't Hurt** \- Freedom from perfectionism; screen to green  
5. **Take Up Space** \- You belong here; reclaim your place in nature

These are what families practice, internalize, and carry home.

---

### **KEY MESSAGES**

**For Families:** "We help families escape isolation—the screens, the exhaustion, the division—through restorative time in nature and practices of belonging. Families discover reconnection they didn't know was possible: to the earth, to themselves, to community across every difference."

**For Funders:** "We address America's isolation epidemic through a proven model organized around 5 circles of wellbeing: Rest, Nourishment, Play, Sacred Space, and Public Lands—all experienced in community across race and class. ### **DIFFERENTIATION**

**You are NOT:**

* A recreation organization ("camping trips")  
* An outdoor education program ("wilderness skills")  
* A diversity initiative ("equity in outdoors")  
* An individual wellness retreat ("self-care in nature")

**You ARE:**

* A reconnection movement using nature as the pathway  
* A community-building practice disguised as camping  
* A wellness model that addresses collective, not just individual, healing  
* A public lands justice initiative experienced through rest and belonging

---

### **WHAT'S DIFFERENT FROM BEFORE**

**Old frame:**

* Lead with: Bridging, democratic renewal, systems change  
* Primary language: john powell, civic infrastructure, cross-class connection  
* Audience: Sophisticated funders who understand bridging theory  
* Risk: Sounds wonky, requires explanation, narrow appeal

**New frame:**

* Lead with: Isolation epidemic, reconnection, 5 circles of wellbeing  
* Primary language: Rest, belonging, sacred space, public lands, nature  
* Audience: All families \+ wellness/mental health funders  
* Benefit: Universal appeal, outcomes still intact but embedded
