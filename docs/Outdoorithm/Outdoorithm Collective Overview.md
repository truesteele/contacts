# **Outdoorithm Collective Overview**

## **Organization Summary**

Outdoorithm Collective transforms access to public lands by creating welcoming, community-driven camping experiences for urban families who are historically underrepresented in outdoor spaces. Through guided group trips and a membership-based collective community, we help families build outdoor confidence, cross-cultural relationships, and lasting connections to nature. Our approach emphasizes shared responsibility and mutual support, bringing diverse communities together on public lands to build something greater than ourselves.

## **Our North Star: The Bridging Model**

**Social cohesion through shared wilderness experiences.** When families from different worlds share the same tent stakes and camp stove, they discover what john powell calls "bridging"—authentic connection across difference that expands everyone's circle of belonging.

Our model: **DEPTH × SCALE**

* **Depth**: 48-hour wilderness trips where status markers dissolve, shared vulnerability emerges, and healing happens  
* **Scale**: Custom technology platform where relationships are sustained, communities self-organize, and the movement grows

In the wilderness, a software engineer teaching a janitor's kid to fish isn't charity—it's connection. A single parent from Oakland helping a Palo Alto family with their tent isn't service—it's solidarity.

## **Vision**

We envision a world where public lands are spaces of deep belonging, where families across America find healing, build generational connections, and discover collective power through immersive outdoor experiences.

## **Mission**

We transform public lands into spaces of belonging by building inclusive camping communities where diverse urban families—particularly those historically excluded from outdoor spaces—find healing, confidence, and authentic connection in nature.

## **Core Values**

**Belonging**: We create spaces where diverse identities and traditions flourish on public lands, ensuring every family feels welcomed, valued, and celebrated in nature.

**Stewardship**: We nurture a lasting commitment to protecting our public lands, ensuring future generations inherit both the natural spaces and the cultural traditions that make them meaningful.

## **Social Context**

At a time of increasing societal fragmentation, camping on public land is one of few activities that naturally brings together Americans across geographic, economic, and racial lines. Data shows that 41% of campers have household incomes under $50,000, they come from urban (25%), suburban (42%), and rural (33%) areas, and while still predominantly white, new campers are increasingly diverse with 34% being people of color.

As john powell teaches, "belonging" isn't about fitting in—it's about co-creating spaces where everyone can show up as their full selves. Parks provide what researchers call an "equigenic effect"—reducing health inequalities by benefiting disadvantaged groups the most.

**Communal Equipment**

* Heated community tent for gathering  
* Full camp kitchen setup  
* Group cooking equipment  
* Shared lighting systems  
* Community dining space

**Camping Tools & Resources**

* Essential camping tools  
* First aid supplies  
* Basic repair equipment  
* Camp setup guides

This equipment approach:

* Removes initial cost barriers  
* Allows families to try camping before investing  
* Creates comfortable experiences that build confidence  
* Demonstrates what equipment is truly essential  
* Provides models for future personal gear decisions

Through our partnership with Outdoorithm, we provide comprehensive gear recommendations, detailed packing guides, and access to discounted equipment through affiliate partnerships for families ready to invest in their own gear. Families bring personal items like sleeping bags and clothing, with detailed guidance provided on what to pack.

**Technology Platform: Community Infrastructure for Connection**

Built by our co-founder Justin Steele (former Google.org director), Outdoorithm Collective operates two integrated technology platforms that work together to transform how families connect with nature and each other.

### **Outdoorithm: The Gateway to Nature**

As part of our commitment to sustainable impact, Outdoorithm Collective has acquired the technology assets of Outdoorithm LLC—the camping discovery and planning platform originally co-founded by our board leadership. This strategic integration brings proven technology directly under our nonprofit mission, ensuring that digital tools serve community building rather than profit maximization.

The platform addresses the fundamental barrier between camping interest and action: the confidence gap. While 34.9 million American families express interest in camping, most never take that first trip due to overwhelming planning complexity, gear confusion, and uncertainty about where they belong in outdoor spaces.

**Outward-Facing Platform Features:**

* **Comprehensive Campground Intelligence**: 11,438 public campgrounds with 1.5M+ analyzed reviews  
* **AI-Powered Trip Planning**: Camp Sage assistant and EarlyBird booking optimization  
* **The Family Camping Path**: 30-day guided journey from camping-curious to first successful trip  
* **Gear Recommendations**: Curated equipment guides integrated with ethical retail partnerships

**Market Impact**: The platform brings 25,000 monthly active users, established REI partnerships, and affiliate revenue streams that help sustain our community programs.

### **Community Platform: Nurturing Connection**

Our internal community management system is more than software—it's community architecture designed to nurture the relationships that wilderness experiences begin.

**Inward-Facing Community Features:**

* **Onboarding as Storytelling**: Families share their hopes and stories, not just contact information  
* **Skills Exchange**: Cross-class skill sharing that builds mutual respect and breaks down barriers  
* **Celebration Rituals**: Milestone recognition for first fish caught, first solo tent setup, first cross-cultural friendship made  
* **Gentle Nudges**: "Maria hasn't heard from you in a while" rather than addictive notifications  
* **Trip Organization**: Member-led adventure planning and coordination tools

### **Integrated Impact**

These platforms work synergistically to serve our mission:

**Discovery to Community**: Families discover our programs through Outdoorithm's campground search, then join our community for supported first experiences.

**Community to Independence**: Collective participants use Outdoorithm's tools to plan their own adventures, equipped with skills and confidence gained through our programs.

**Data-Driven Programming**: Platform insights inform which campgrounds and regions to feature in our community programming, ensuring we meet families where their interests already lie.

**Sustainable Operations**: Affiliate revenue from authentic gear recommendations helps support our community programs while maintaining mission alignment.

The platforms don't create connection—they protect and nurture what the wilderness begins, helping relationships deepen between trips and enabling families to self-organize their own adventures. By bringing both the discovery engine and community infrastructure under our nonprofit umbrella, we ensure technology amplifies rather than replaces human connection, creating seamless pathways from outdoor curiosity to confident, connected outdoor engagement.
