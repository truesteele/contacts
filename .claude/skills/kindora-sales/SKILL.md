# Kindora Sales Email Context

Load Justin's Kindora sales email guide and email persona for crafting responses to new users, inbound interest, warm intros, follow-ups, and other sales-related emails from the justin@kindora.co account.

## Required: Always load both documents
@../../docs/Best_Practices/KINDORA_SALES_EMAIL_GUIDE.md
@../../docs/Justin/JUSTIN_EMAIL_PERSONA.md

## Instructions

After loading the guides above:

1. **Identify the email type** using the buyer types and templates in the sales guide (Part 4 and Part 5)
2. **Recommend the right template** and explain why it fits
3. **Flag any voice rules** from both guides that are especially relevant (e.g., no em dashes, no "just following up", Calendly link choice, sign-off style)
4. **Draft a response** in Justin's voice, following both the sales guide structure and the persona guide tone
5. **Run the AI Writing Avoidance Checklist** (Part 7 of sales guide) before presenting the draft

When the sales guide and persona guide overlap, the sales guide takes precedence for Kindora-specific emails (it has stricter rules, e.g., never use em dashes). The persona guide fills in broader voice patterns the sales guide doesn't cover.

Always present the draft for Justin's review before sending. Ask if he wants to tweak anything.
