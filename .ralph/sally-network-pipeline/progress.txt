### Iteration 3 — US-003: Import SMS Conversations (2026-03-09)
**Status:** COMPLETE
**What was done:**
- Created `scripts/intelligence/sally/import_sms.py`
- Reads `docs/Sally/sms_parsed.json` (1,230 phone number entries)

**Results:** 80 conversations matched, 22,611 total messages, 0 errors

**Learnings:**
- 214 phone numbers had duplicate formats in the parsed JSON — merging is essential
- 314 unmatched entries are mostly family nicknames (Mom, Krizol, Kim), first-name-only contacts, or people not on LinkedIn
- Fuzzy matching catches hyphenated name differences (Kay Fernandez-Smith ↔ Kay Fernandez Smith) and middle initial differences (Natasha Logan ↔ Natasha L. Logan)
